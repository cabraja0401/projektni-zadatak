Vinicius tests negative, while Courtois, Benzema and Mendy miss training

Carlo Ancelotti will be glad that Vinicius has returned a negative COVID-19 test and will soon be able to re-join first-team training and matches.

However, Real Madrid's Valdebebas training ground is still missing a number of famous faces the day before their Copa del Rey fixture against Alcoyano.

Dani Carvajal and Gareth Bale have been ruled out in recent days due to their respective injury concerns, but should be available to feature in upcoming games against Valencia (January 8) and the all-important Supercopa de Espana semi-final against Barcelona (January 12).

On Tuesday, Thibaut Courtois, Ferland Mendy and Karim Benzema all missed training for unknown reasons.
