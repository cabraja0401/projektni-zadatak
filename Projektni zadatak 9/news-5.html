Haaland revives the war between Real Madrid and Barcelona

Ironically, Real Madrid and Barcelona both want the same thing, Florentino Perez and Joan Laporta both want the same thing, and that is to secure the signing of Erling Haaland.

On the pitch the height of the rivalry came during the Jose Mourinho and Pep Guardiola era, with some of the developments in Clasico matches being nothing short of a disgrace, yet off the pitch there is now going to be a battle to sign the Borussia Dortmund star.

The German club are desperate for the 21-year-old to make a clear decision on his future before the end of January, not because they want to lose him immediately but rather they want to kickstart their planning for the 2022/23 season in the knowledge of how much money they will have to spend.

Laporta is known to be a cold operator in the transfer market - you have to remember that he has already met with Mino Raiola on two occasions - and he was able to sign Ferran Torres under the cover of night when everyone believed the deal to be dead.

Real Madrid and Florentino Perez are operating a little more openly, as they have made no secret of the fact that their number one target is Paris Saint-Germain forward Kylian Mbappe, who they plan to sign on a Bosman deal on July 1.
